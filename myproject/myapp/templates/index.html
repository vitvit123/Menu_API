Our story

from 2017

Ichiruka was born out of a vision to provide a simple, yet warm and comfortable dining experience. It all started with an idea of two childhood friends, baseball teammates and food lovers, to bring together each of their own strengths, to create something they can share with others. With Chef Krsna – a natural artist, with an immense passion in culinary art, and Kai – a CPA accountant with business acumen, Ichiruka Restaurant was born.

Ichiruka’ is your place to enjoy good food with the comfort and familiarity of home! Ichiruka’ was born out of a vision to provide you a simple yet warm and comfortable dining experience!
